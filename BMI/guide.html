Guidelines for maintaining BMI

BMI < 18.5

UNDER WEIGHT

To improve your health:

  • Eat Nutrient-rich foods
  • Do Exercise regularly
  • Add healthy calories
  • Eat Healthy Snack
  • Eat More Fruits, Vegetables, Whole Grains

BMI 18.5 >= 24.9

NORMAL

You are in Normal Health. To maintain this normal BMI, you should exercise regularly, eat healthy foods and drink plenty of water.

BMI 25.0 >= 29.9

OVER WEIGHT

To Reduce Over weight:

  • Choose weight-loss-friendly foods
  • Eat a high protein breakfast
  • Eat soluble fiber

BMI > 29.9

OBESITY

To reduce Obesity:

  • Exercise regularly
  • Monitor your weight regularly
  • Be consistent
  • Avoid the food traps that cause you to eat

Losing weight is hard, maintaining weight is hard, staying overweight is hard.

Choose your hard!
